## Key Research Insights

- **QUIC connection migration:** 76% failure rate on hard handoffs (Wi-Fi→cellular). Expect failures in O6. Document fallback (reconnect + session resumption).
- **BBR fairness:** BBRv3 can grab 99% bandwidth vs Cubic. Use Cubic for agent-to-agent RPC, BBR for relay forwarding.
- **Only 52% of IPv4 QUIC servers support connection migration.** This is a known industry issue.
